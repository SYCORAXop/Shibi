Our Journey

From a Needle & Thread to a Beloved Boutique

✦

Ananya Boutique was born from a simple love — the love of creating something beautiful with one's own hands. What started as stitching clothes for family and friends gradually grew into a trusted home boutique serving hundreds of women across Mumbai and beyond.

With over 8 years of tailoring experience, our founder Ananya brings a designer's eye and a craftsperson's precision to every piece. She learnt the art of stitching from her mother, and has since refined it into a professional practice that blends traditional Indian techniques with modern silhouettes.

Today, Ananya Boutique specialises in custom women's clothing — from everyday kurtis to elegant blouses and statement dresses — all stitched at home with a commitment to quality that you simply can't find in mass-produced fashion.
