Common Questions

FAQs

How do I share my measurements?

After placing your order, we'll send a measurement guide over WhatsApp. You can share them via message or we can schedule a brief video call.

How long does stitching take?

Standard orders: 7–10 working days. Custom or embroidery work: 12–15 working days. Bridal wear: 3–4 weeks. We'll confirm at the time of order.

Can I provide my own fabric?

Absolutely! Many customers send us their own fabric. We can also help you source quality fabric based on your design requirements.

Do you offer alterations?

Yes, we offer alterations for garments we've stitched as well as existing clothes you'd like resized or restyled. Contact us with details.
